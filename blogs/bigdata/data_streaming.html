Step 1. Open a terminal shell. Open a terminal shell by clicking on the square black box on the top left of the screen. >> cd Downloads/big-data-2/sensor

Step 2. View streaming weather station data. Run stream-data.py to see streaming data from the weather station: >> ./stream-data.py
